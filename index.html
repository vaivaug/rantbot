What did we do?

We made an app designed to talk you through your problems and clear your mind. After a stressful day of work, or a bad breakup, or even if you're just upset about the state of US politics, telling someone about it can make all the difference. We wanted to teach robots to understand emotion, so that removing that cloud from over your head becomes that bit easier.

Why did we do it?
